About me

I am a Computing with Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence Student at Technological University Dublin with a strong interest in software development, data systems, and problem-solving. Over the past few years, I have worked across a wide range of projects — from web applications and analytics dashboards to distributed systems and machine learning concepts. Alongside my technical work, I have developed strong teamwork, communication, and customer-facing skills through various roles in hospitality, technology, and client services. I enjoy learning new tools and technologies, and I am particularly drawn to projects that blend creativity with technical depth. Outside of tech, I am passionate about volleyball, cars and motorsport, and creating meaningful user experiences through thoughtful design.
